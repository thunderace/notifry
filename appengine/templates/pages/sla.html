Notifry is a free service. As such, there is no guarantee that the service will work at any given time, as many different technologies and systems are involved, and any interuption in that chain will stop the system from working. For example, the Google App Engine backend could exhaust its assigned resources, or the C2DM APIs could exceed their limits.

Notifry will make the best effort to deliver your message, but we can not be held accountable for any failure to notify a user via the service.

It is recommended to back up any Notifry system with an alternate notification system, such as SMS or email, depending on your desired urgency level.
